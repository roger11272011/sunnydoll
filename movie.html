I'M

人們把祈求雨停的希望加注在晴天娃娃身上，如果沒有作用，則會抱怨晴天娃娃是“沒用的”，但其實到最後不論雨有沒有停，晴天娃娃都會被丟棄。就像在某方面的思想被他人灌輸，每個人都活成一個樣子，如同樣貌相同、無色彩的晴天娃娃，被名為期待的繩子緊緊地綁住，把目光面向大家所期待的方向，而最後被丟棄的則是自我思考能力。

在劇裡共有兩隻晴天娃娃，主角晴天娃娃在意識到問題的時候，會試圖想方法，並奮力地去掙脫，而另一隻晴天娃娃，代表著害怕與他人不同，寧願永遠無生命、無情緒的被控制，我們將兩隻角色在動畫中的行為，呈現出相反的對比，進而導出結局的自由意識，讓有相同困境的人們能有帶入感，並從動畫中感受到共鳴。

People place their hopes for the rain to stop on the Sunny Doll. If it doesn't work, they'll complain that the Sunny Doll is "useless". But in the end, whether the rain stops or not, the Sunny Doll will still be discarded. Just like how thoughts in certain aspects are instilled by others, everyone ends up living the same, like colorless Sunny Dolls with identical appearances, tightly bound by the rope called expectation, facing the direction anticipated by everyone. Eventually, what gets discarded is the ability for independent thinking.

In the play, there are two Sunny Dolls. The main Sunny Doll, upon realizing the problem, tries to find solutions and struggles to break free. The other Sunny Doll, representing the fear of being different from others, prefers to be forever lifeless and emotionless, controlled by others. We present the contrasting behaviors of these two characters in the animation, leading to the theme of liberation in the end, allowing people with similar struggles to empathize and resonate with the animation.
